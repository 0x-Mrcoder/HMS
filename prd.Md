Complete Concept & Features of the Hospital Management System (HMS)

### **With Integrated Patient Wallet**

---

## **1. Overview of the System**

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a fully integrated software designed to manage all hospital operations digitally. It connects every department—Reception, Consulting, Pharmacy, Lab, Nursing, NHIS, OPD/IPD, Accounts, and Management—into one unified platform.

The system includes a **Patient Wallet**, allowing patients to pre-fund their accounts and enabling automatic, transparent deductions for all services received.

---

## **2. Core Goals of the HMS**

* Modernize and automate all hospital workflows.
* Reduce queues, delays, and manual errors.
* Ensure transparent billing through a unified patient wallet.
* Improve communication between departments.
* Provide real-time reporting for management decision-making.
* Prevent revenue leakage and unauthorized service consumption.
* Strengthen data security and access control.

---

# **3. Full System Features**

---

# **A. Patient Management Module**

### **Features:**

* Patient registration with auto-generated unique ID.
* Creation of digital patient file (EMR).
* Patient demographic details: name, age, gender, contact, address.
* Medical history: allergies, past treatments, conditions.
* Visit history and clinical notes stored securely.
* Quick patient search by name, phone, ID, or card number.

---

# **B. Patient Wallet System**

### **Features:**

* Automatic creation of wallet upon registration.
* Wallet can be funded via:

  * Cash
  * POS payment
  * Bank transfer
  * Online payment (if cloud-enabled)
* Real-time wallet balance visible to authorized staff.
* Automatic deduction of charges for:

  * Consultation
  * Laboratory tests
  * Drugs from pharmacy
  * Nursing procedures
  * Theatre/surgical operations
  * Admission (bed space) fees
* Low-balance warnings.
* Refund option for discharged patients.
* Full transaction history for transparency.

---

# **C. Consulting / Doctor’s Module**

### **Features:**

* Doctors can only open patient file if wallet meets minimum balance.
* View patient history, vitals, and past visits.
* Write prescriptions digitally.
* Order laboratory tests.
* See real-time lab results.
* View nursing notes and progress updates.
* Update diagnosis and treatment plan.
* Ability to refer patient to another department.

---

# **D. Pharmacy Module**

### **Features:**

* See prescriptions directly from consulting rooms.
* Confirm wallet balance before dispensing.
* Auto-deduct drug costs from wallet.
* Pharmacy stock management:

  * Stock in/out
  * Expiry tracking
  * Low-stock alerts
* Mark prescription as:

  * Pending
  * Dispensed
  * Rejected (with reason)
* Daily pharmacy revenue report.

---

# **E. Laboratory & Diagnostics Module**

### **Features:**

* Lab receives test orders from doctors.
* Check wallet balance before processing test.
* Deduct test charges automatically.
* Record test results with notes, attachments, images.
* Notify doctor when results are ready.
* Lab revenue tracking and daily summary.

---

# **F. Nursing / Ward Management Module**

### **Features:**

* Nurses view all treatment plans assigned by doctors.
* Record vital signs: BP, temp, pulse, SPO2, etc.
* Update patient activities:

  * “Medication given”
  * “IV drip started”
  * “Dressing done”
  * “Monitoring completed”
* Timestamp for every action.
* Bed allocation management for inpatient.
* Nurses can escalate issues to doctors with urgent alerts.

---

# **G. Theatre / Surgical Module**

### **Features:**

* Surgeons view patient file & wallet balance before operation.
* Log surgical procedures and outcomes.
* Record consumables used (cotton, sutures, instruments).
* Automatically deduct surgical fees and consumables from wallet.
* Assign post-surgery tasks to nurses.
* Pharmacy can get auto-request for theatre drugs.

---

# **H. NHIS / Insurance Module**

### **Features:**

* Verify NHIS eligibility.
* Process claims and approvals.
* Handle co-pay (wallet + insurance split).
* Track insured patient records.
* Export NHIS monthly claim reports.

---

# **I. Accounts & Billing Module**

### **Features:**

* Complete financial dashboard.
* Track all payments received via:

  * Wallet
  * Cash
  * POS
  * Bank transfers
* Audit trail for all transactions.
* Generate invoices and receipts.
* Refund processing.
* Generate financial reports:

  * Daily income
  * Weekly revenue
  * Monthly reports by department

---

# **J. Management & Administration Module**

### **Features:**

* Central dashboard showing:

  * Number of patients
  * Revenue
  * Drugs low in stock
  * Pending lab tests
  * Staff activities
* Access control:

  * Admin controls permissions
  * Create/edit user roles
  * Block/activate staff accounts
* Audit logs:

  * Every action tracked
  * Who accessed what, when

---

# **K. Security Features**

### **Features:**

* Role-based access control
* Encrypted patient records
* Secure login & password policy
* Audit logging
* Backup system
* Multi-branch support (if cloud or hybrid)

---

# **4. System Advantages**

* Faster patient processing.
* No lost files — everything digital.
* Eliminates revenue leakage.
* Automated billing.
* Better communication between departments.
* Fewer errors and safer medication process.
* Strong reporting for management.
